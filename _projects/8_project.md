*The Parallax View* was a weekly radio program on WRUU 107.5FM in Savannah, GA (2021–2023), engaging listeners on the design, urbanism, and civic life shaping their built environment. The program examined how architecture and urban decisions affect everyday experience, bringing critical design thinking to a public audience outside the academy.
