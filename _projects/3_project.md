The CLOUD Collaborative is a practice-based research initiative treating atmospheric clouds as a vehicle for exploring computational form, representation, and metaphor. Using Python and Blender with the Alembic simulation standard, the project investigates how weather systems and volumetric phenomena can inform architectural thinking about space, structure, and image.

Work in 2025 includes co-authorship of the cataloging framework and a public contributor call, expanding the collaborative's reach to practitioners and researchers working at the intersection of computation and natural systems.
