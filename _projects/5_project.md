As the founding architect of NCCU's Pre-Architecture Concentration, this ongoing program development effort represents the first pre-architecture curriculum established at an HBCU in North Carolina. Work encompasses curriculum design, accreditation planning, student recruitment, and the development of a full B.Arch degree proposal currently under review by the UNC System Board of Governors.

The concentration prepares students for graduate-level professional architecture programs while building a pipeline of architecture students from historically underrepresented backgrounds — advancing equity in a profession that remains among the least diverse of the design disciplines.
