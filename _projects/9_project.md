Early practice work at Burke, Hogue & Mills Associates, Inc. (Lake Mary, FL, 1998–2003) included historic preservation and rehabilitation projects recognized by the Florida Trust for Historic Preservation. Projects included the Tarragona Arch and the Daytona Beach Bandshell (both receiving Florida Trust awards for outstanding achievement in restoration and rehabilitation), as well as the Lakeview Middle School Historic Restoration in Winter Garden, FL — recipient of the ABC Excellence in Construction Eagle Award in the $1–$5 million category.
