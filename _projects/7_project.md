Co-host and lead designer on _Fix It & Finish It_, a nationally syndicated home renovation television program. Episodes filmed in Savannah, GA aired as part of the show's broader run across the country, bringing design expertise and architectural thinking to a mass audience through accessible, project-based storytelling.
