Prof. Singeisen has been awarded a $10,000 NCCU Sponsored Research Grant for "Empowering Architectural Education Through Artificial Intelligence: A Pilot Initiative at North Carolina Central University," running July 2025 through June 2026.

---

#### About the Project

The project develops and tests AI-integrated approaches to architectural pedagogy at NCCU, with the goal of producing a replicable model for design education at HBCUs and comparable institutions. The initiative builds on Singeisen's ongoing engagement with digital tools in beginning design education and connects to his broader research on representation and design thinking.

The grant is administered through NCCU's Office of Sponsored Research.
