Singeisen's generative video work _In the Sky: CLOUDS_ (2025) was included in the group exhibition _In the Sky: Clouds Lent Out Use Discipline_ at the Ewing Gallery, University of Tennessee Knoxville, October 27 – December 2, 2025.

---

#### About the Work

_In the Sky: CLOUDS_ is a 40-second generative video created in Python and Blender, treating atmospheric clouds as a vehicle for exploring computational form, representation, and metaphor. The work is part of Singeisen's CLOUD Collaborative Research Project, an ongoing practice-based initiative in which monthly contributions explore the cloud as a model for thinking about form, process, and digital image-making.
