Prof. Singeisen presented "The Influence of Fine Art Printmaking on Architectural Image Production and Reception" at the 113th Annual Conference of the College Art Association (CAA) in New York, NY.

---

#### About the Paper

The paper examines an underexplored intersection of architectural history and art history: how fine art printmaking shaped the production and public reception of architectural images. The research investigates the role of artistic rendering — lithography in particular — in mediating how architecture is seen, circulated, and understood. The work is part of Singeisen's ongoing research into the relationship between printmaking and architectural representation.

CAA is the leading scholarly organization for the visual arts and art history in higher education.
