The NCCU Pre-Architecture Concentration has been included in the AIA Triangle Educational Scholarship Program beginning Fall 2026, with an anticipated first-year award of $4,000 for NCCU students. The partnership also established AIA Triangle Women in Architecture awards for eligible female NCCU students.
