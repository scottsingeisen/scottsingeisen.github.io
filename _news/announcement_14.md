Singeisen's generative video work _No Two Are the Same_ (2025) was included in the Department of Art & Design Faculty Exhibition at the NCCU Museum of Art, Durham, NC, February 10 – March 13, 2026.

---

#### About the Work

_No Two Are the Same_ is a 40-second generative video created in Python and Blender. The title invokes the essential nature of clouds — each one singular, each one the product of the same underlying conditions — as a way of thinking about form, difference, and algorithmic process. The work continues Singeisen's CLOUD Collaborative Research Project, an ongoing practice-based initiative exploring computational form, representation, and metaphor through the study of atmospheric phenomena.
